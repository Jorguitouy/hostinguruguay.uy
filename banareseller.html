  • What is reseller hosting?
    Reseller hosting, sometimes referred to as white label hosting, allows you to create your own web hosting company without having to incur the costs of server maintenance, software, site uptime, and more. Reseller hosting is perfect for freelancers or agencies offering web design, development, and other business services to their clients. With reseller hosting, clients purchase and host their websites through you as if you were the hosting company.
  • How do I transfer my website to BanaHosting?
    If you’ve built your website in a HTML editor (like Dreamweaver or Microsoft Expression Studio), upload your website files via FTP (File Transfer Protocol). We have a built-in FTP File Manager for you to access in our Hosting Control Center. If your files are larger than 20 MB, we recommend using the tool FileZilla, compatible with Windows®, Mac®, and Linux® operating systems. Or, you may choose to use third-party FTP clients. We offer free cPanel to cPanel migration.
  • What is WordPress?
    As the most popular tool to create websites, it’s a content management system (CMS) that develops various types of websites.
  • Which control panel comes with my hosting account?
    All Our web hosting, Reseller and Semi-Dedicated plans gives you free cPanel control Panel included to manage your hosting account. cPanel provides you tools to help you run your website easy. You can manage your domains, sub-domains, emails, file management etc. with your cPanel.
